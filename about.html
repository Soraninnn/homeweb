ABOUT US

Who We Are

Serves a target group, Non-Profit Organization

HEART OF MENTORING (H.o.ME) is an NGO which aims to serve, build up, and mentor young adults aging out of the shelters in Mongolia as well as be the support and bridge connecting them to their future communities, preparing them to become the people God intends them to be.

Our Mission

Empowering at-risk young adults and raising them to become future generations of mentors, founded in faith by:

  • EMBRACING
  • CONNECTING
  • PROVIDING
  • ENCOURAGING
  • GUIDING
  • CHALLENGING
Our Vision

A world where Christ-centered,
life-long mentorship is displayed among generations.

Core Values

Christ-centered

Jesus Christ is the head of H.o.ME, and
the Word of God is the ultimate guide.

Partnership

H.o.M.E. collaborates with mentees, local and international supporters, volunteers, churches, and para-churches to work towards shared goals.

Empowerment

H.o.ME creates opportunities for mentees to explore, take responsibility for, own the consequences, and grow from their decisions.

Team-oriented mindset

H.o.ME cultivates a culture that prioritizes collective open communication, collaboration, mutual respect, and support.

Shared leadership

H.o.ME values diverse perspectives, shared accountability, and decisions made through prayer and agreement.

Relationship

H.o.ME is committed to building meaningful and transformative relationships between mentors and mentees, within the community, and with God.

Focus area

Christ-Centered Mentorship

We walk alongside mentees through intentional, faith-based mentorship that helps them grow in Christ and invest in others.

Housing

We provide a safe housing environment that gives mentees a foundation to thrive and build their future.

Resilience

We provide emotional, spiritual, and physical care through professional counseling, intentional prayer, and holistic support, promoting personal growth and overall well-being.

Community & Connection

We foster authentic relationships and Christ-centered communities where mentees can belong, grow, and serve together.

Education

We support personal and professional development by providing access to learning opportunities, life skills, and spiritual formation.
